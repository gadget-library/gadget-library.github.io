Our story

Rural education in India has always been a challenge since independence. A country’s development hugely relies on the literacy rate of urban and rural areas .The destitute people in villages do not have enough money to attend schools and the schools located in rural areas are also not equipped with enough support to help the child grow. With the spread of Covid-19 the urban school/college students are also forced to sit at home and study. The gap between the rural and urban students has drastically decreased. The only gap that remains is the gadgets and gizmos that allow the students to learn anytime and at any corner of the country. A willing child can learn anywhere with just a mere smart phone in his hand and an internet connection in it. Our ORGANISATION deals with this problem and solving it with the easiest yet efficient solution. In a similar way, our organization named ‘GADGET LIBRARY’ should be setup in rural areas that can Collect very meager amount of money from the rural section of society and promise to provide not only gadgets like laptops ,tablets and smart phones but also internet connections to the student community who are willing to learn and get educated.
